Full-Time Retail Assistant

Closing date 25 June 2022
We are seeking an additional member of staff to fit into our small and friendly team.
If you are looking for a career in retail then this could be the ideal position for you.
Training opportunities are available to Assistant Manager level.
    Your key duties will be:
  • Provide high levels of customer service at all times
  • Operating the till and stock management systems
  • Updating our Social Media pages and website with current promotions
  • Carry out stock replenishment, ensuring all areas of the store remain tidy
  • Work as an active member of the team, supporting colleagues in all tasks
  • Meeting high standards of display and visual merchandising to ensure the store is well presented at all times

So, if you are reliable, competent, positive, motivated and you like the sound of taking on a key role within our small and friendly team then please contact us.
